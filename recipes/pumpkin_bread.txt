Pumpkin Bread (with Dried Cherries)

(Modified from Betty Crocker Zuchinni Bread with Raisins Recipe)

1 Can (15 oz) of Pumpkin 
1/2 & 1/3 cups of sugar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspooon of vanilla
2 eggs
1.5 cups of all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup dried cherries

Move oven racks down to stop of loaves will be in center of oven. Preheat to 350 degrees.

Grease bottom of pans with shortening or cooking spray.

Mix pumpkin, sugar, oil, vanilla, and eggs until mixed.

Stir in remaining ingredients except cherries, then stir in cherries. 

Divide batter among loaf pans, bake loaves for 45-60 mins. Cool on wire rack.
