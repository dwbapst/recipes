Honey Wheat Bread

Amounts in parentheses is for two loaves.

1 & 1/8  teaspoon active dry yeast (2 & 1/4 tsp)
1 cup warm water (2 cups)
3 tablespoons honey or dark molasses (1/3 cups)
1 tablespoon shortening (2 tablespoons)
2 teaspoons salt (4 tsp)

Mix these ingredients in bowl. Then slowly add flour to make stiff dough:

2.25 to 3 cups whole wheat flour (5.5 to 6 cups)

Knead dough well on floured surface until smooth and satiny (8 to 10 minutes). 

Place in greased bowl and cover. Let rise in warm place until double, 1.5 to 2 hours.

(Divide in half.) Shape into balls. Cover with bowl, let rest 15 minutes. 

Shape into loaves. Place into well-greased 9x5 pans. Cover. Let rise until doubled, about 1 hour.

Bake at 375 degrees F, for 30 to 35 minutes or until deep golden brown.
