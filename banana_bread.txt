Banana Bread
(Betty Crocker Cookbook)

1 & 1/4 cups sugar
1/2     cup butter or margerine, softened (1 stick)
2       large eggs
1 & 1/2 cups mashed very ripe bananas
1/2     cup buttermilk
1       teaspoon vanilla
2 & 1/2 cups All-Purpose Flour
1       teaspoon baking soda
1       teaspoon salt

1) Move oven rack to low position so that tops of pans will be in center of pan. Heat oven to 350 deg F.

2) Grease bottoms of three small loaf pans

3) In large bowl, stir sugar and butter until well mixed. 

4) Stir in eggs until well-mixed. 

5) Stir in bananas, buttermilk and vanilla; beat with spoon until smooth.

6) Stir in flour, baking soda and salt until moistened.

7) Divide batter among loaf pans and bake, checking after about 45 minutes.

8) Loosen loafs from pans and let cool top-side up on wire rack for 1-2 hours.
